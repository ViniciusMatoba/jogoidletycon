## OpenGameArt - Isometric 64x64 Medieval Building Tileset

- Source: https://opengameart.org/content/isometric-64x64-medieval-building-tileset
- Author: Seth Galbraith
- License listed by source: CC-BY 3.0
- Local files:
  - `opengameart/isometric-64x64-medieval-building-tileset/iso-64x64-building.png`
- Best use in this project: reference/prototype for compact pixel-art buildings on a square isometric grid.
- Notes: source says it was designed for Yar's isometric 64x64 outside tileset and inspired by Clint Bellanger's Medieval Building Tiles.
